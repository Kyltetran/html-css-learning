Digital Humanities in Vietnam

Digital Humanities is an interdisciplinary field that explores the intersection of digital technologies and humanities scholarship. At its core, Digital Humanities seeks to use computational tools and methods to analyze, interpret, and disseminate cultural artifacts and information, including literature, history, art, and music...
This is an example for Digital Humanities in Vietnam, which combines digital technologies and social issues at the same time with the purpose of protecting Vietnamese culture. People can observe Vietnamese tangible cultural heritage as a digital representation rather than a physical artifact, which is called digitization. On the other hand, it is still digitalization, in which people use digital technology to change the model, from human-driven events to software-driven events.
